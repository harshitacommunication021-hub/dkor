Your Luxury Interior Designers in Miami

Great design begins with you. In 2004, DKOR Interiors was founded by Ivonne Ronderos to provide personalized design services with complete transparency. Over the past twenty years, we’ve flourished into a full-service, award-winning interior design firm thanks to our supportive clients. Working hand-in-hand with you, we design high-quality residential and commercial spaces where you can truly thrive.

Rather than following a single design style, we’re driven by a spirit of creativity, honesty, and collaboration. We listen and we learn. Each project tells a story about you – what you like and how you want to live. While we call Miami home, we work with clients across the United States and internationally. Our team of hardworking, talented interior designers in Miami partners with trusted builders and vendors to ensure every project is handled with care.

Experiencing the DKOR difference means enjoying peace of mind as your vision comes to life – and it’s even better than you imagined!

WHY WORK WITH US?

Clients Are Our Number One Priority

We listen to you, respect your budget, and are always just a call away. We know what works and what doesn’t and we use that knowledge to guide you toward the personalized interior you’ve always imagined. By putting you at the center of everything we do, we customize our design approach to fit your unique goals, creating spaces that are as individual as you are.

Collaborative Team

We believe in the strength of collaboration and work with people who share our commitment to exceptional quality and service. Each one of our team members brings a knowledge of their specific area – it’s not a “one-man show.” We’ve also built strong, trusted relationships with general contractors, subcontractors, and vendors, and because of these connections, we’re able to offer you exclusive benefits and perks.

Transparent Process

We prioritize open and honest conversations between clients, our team, and partners, ensuring transparency throughout every project. Our pricing is straightforward, with no hidden markups or surprises – just honest value. Plus, you’ll benefit from our exclusive discounts, which can really add up in the end!

Creative Designs

Every project includes creative custom solutions that are tailored to each client’s vision, lifestyle, and needs. With expertise that spans a variety of styles, from contemporary to traditional and everything in between, we develop concepts to match your tastes. For us, it’s not just about making a space look beautiful; it’s about making it work for you. With decades of experience, we know how to design timeless interiors that stand the test of time, so you won’t need frequent redecorating. Our goal is to design spaces that remain fresh and timeless for years to come. Above all, your space should reflect who you are – it’s your home, not ours.

When you work with us, you’ll experience a true collaborative effort with efficient processes, full transparency, and communication every step of the way. Let’s build something beautiful together!
